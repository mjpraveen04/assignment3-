Workshops

Department of Information Science and Engineering had conducted Two days workshop on “Robotics and its Aplication” on 5th and 6th September 2019 for Final year students. Morning session was dealt on “Applications of Robotics” by Resource person Mr. Harish N (Chief Technology Officer, Karunadu Technolgy Pvt.Ltd, Bengaluru) and Mr. Mahesh Deginal (Managing Director, Karunadu Technology Pvt.Ltd, Bengaluru). The afternoon session was on hands-on session using Arduino and its Applications, which was efficiently held in our laboratories. A total of 34 students and Faculty members participated in this workshop.


Cultural Events

Sri Krishna Institute of Technology, Bangalore conducted five days Induction Program (Phase 1) for the First year students of BE degree course from 16 August, 2019 to 21 August, 2019 The aim of the program was to give the students joining the college a bird’s eye view about various dimensions of Engineering and the career opportunities for the engineering students and to acquaint them with the systems and procedures of the college. The motive of the program was to give proper orientation to the new students about the course and future prospects and the methodology of learning to be adopted to be successful in life.
